    3D Building using 2D GANs - Christopher Tao

    In the near future, many of our heritage face the risk of being destroyed and fading into the shadows of history. And despite there being a huge effort involved in fighting for the preservation and protection of such heritage sites, I have also explored the realm of preserving our heritage in a digital form, and I believe that this paper can inspire and motivate others into the field of using computer science in cultural preservation.
